OUR MISSION
We are Reef and Randy from Rodney College, and we're on a mission to spread awareness about rubbish pollution in the Kaipara District through our website. As Year 13 students, we're passionate about preserving our environment and raising awareness about the impact of waste on our community.

Our goal is simple yet crucial: to educate, inspire, and mobilise individuals and businesses to adopt sustainable practices. Through our website, we aim to provide valuable resources, insightful articles, and practical tips on reducing, reusing, and recycling.

Join us in our journey to make a difference. Together, we can create a cleaner, greener future for the Kaipara District and beyond. Let's turn awareness into action and waste into wisdom with Waste Wise!
Waste Wise

Established in 2024, Waste Wise is a project ran by Reef and Randy from Rodney College, and we're on a mission to spread awareness of rubbish pollution in the Kaipara region, Rodney College, and New Zealand! We're passionate about spreading awareness of this issue.
